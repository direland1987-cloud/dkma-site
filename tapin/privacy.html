Tapin Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 2025
Last Updated: January 2025

1. Introduction

Tapin ("we," "our," "us," or "Company") operates the Tapin mobile application (the "App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience on our App. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our App.

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our App.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide Directly

Account Information:

  • Email address
  • Password (encrypted)
  • User profile information (name, belt level, gym affiliation)

Training Data:

  • Lesson notes and descriptions
  • Training focus areas (technique, strength, conditioning)
  • Attendance records and check-in history
  • Personal training metrics and progress tracking

User-Generated Content:

  • Community posts and comments
  • Direct messages between users
  • Photos and media attachments

Biometric Data:

  • Face ID data (iOS) or facial recognition data (Android)
  • Important: Biometric data is processed and stored locally on your device only. We do not collect, store, or transmit your biometric data to our servers.

Device Information:

  • Device type and model
  • Operating system and version
  • Unique device identifier
  • IP address
  • Log data and app usage analytics

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

Usage Analytics:

  • Features accessed and frequency of use
  • Time spent in the App
  • Crash reports and error logs
  • Device permissions granted

Cookies and Similar Technologies:

  • Session tokens for authentication
  • Preferences and settings
  • Anonymous analytics tracking

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Account Management: Creating and maintaining your user account
  • Service Delivery: Providing training tracking, note-taking, and community features
  • Communication: Sending push notifications, maintenance updates, and support responses
  • Analytics & Improvement: Understanding user behavior to enhance App features and performance
  • Security: Detecting and preventing fraud, unauthorized access, and security threats
  • Legal Compliance: Fulfilling legal obligations and responding to lawful requests
  • Personalization: Customizing your experience based on preferences and activity

4. Data Processing and Supabase

4.1 Supabase as Data Processor

We use Supabase (an open-source Firebase alternative) as our primary backend infrastructure and data processor. Your data is processed and stored on Supabase's servers.

Data Security Measures:

  • End-to-end encryption for sensitive data (passwords, authentication tokens)
  • HTTPS/TLS encryption in transit
  • Database-level encryption at rest
  • Regular security audits and compliance certifications

Supabase Compliance:

  • GDPR compliant data processing
  • SOC 2 Type II certified
  • EU and US data center options
  • Standard Data Processing Agreement in place

4.2 Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to provide our services:

  • Account Data: Retained while your account is active and 90 days after deletion
  • Training Notes & Posts: Retained until you delete them or request account deletion
  • Analytics Data: Retained for up to 12 months
  • Backups: Retained in compliance with Supabase retention policies

Upon account deletion, all personal data is permanently removed from our active systems within 30 days, except where legally required to retain records.

5. Sharing Your Information

5.1 We Do Not Sell Your Data

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties.

5.2 Shared With

Your information may be shared in the following circumstances:

Community Features:

  • Your posts and comments are visible to other App users (as per your privacy settings)
  • Your gym name and belt level may be visible on your profile
  • Your training notes are private by default unless you choose to share them

Service Providers:

  • Supabase (data processor)
  • Third-party analytics services (anonymized data only)
  • Push notification service providers

Legal Requirements:

  • Law enforcement or government agencies when required by law
  • To protect our legal rights and the rights of users
  • In case of a data breach, to comply with notification requirements

5.3 Data Sharing Controls

You have control over what information you share:

  • Adjust privacy settings to control post visibility
  • Delete content at any time
  • Disable push notifications
  • Opt-out of analytics tracking

6. Biometric Data & Face ID

6.1 Local Processing Only

Face ID (iOS) and biometric authentication features are processed entirely on your device. Your biometric data is NOT:

  • Transmitted to our servers
  • Stored in any cloud database
  • Shared with third parties
  • Used for purposes other than device authentication

6.2 Apple & Android Privacy

Your biometric data is protected by Apple's and Android's own security frameworks:

  • Biometric information is managed by the device's secure enclave
  • We only receive authentication success/failure signals
  • We never access or see your actual biometric data

7. Push Notifications

7.1 Push Notification Data

We may send you push notifications about:

  • Lesson reminders and schedule updates
  • Community activity (new posts, comments, messages)
  • Account security alerts
  • App updates and maintenance notices

7.2 Opting Out

You can disable push notifications at any time through:

  • App settings (in-app notification preferences)
  • Device settings (iOS: Settings > Notifications > Tapin; Android: Settings > Apps > Tapin > Notifications)

8. Your Privacy Rights (GDPR & Similar Laws)

If you are located in the European Union, California, Australia, or other jurisdictions with privacy laws, you have the following rights:

8.1 Access & Portability

You have the right to access your personal data and request a copy in a portable format.

8.2 Correction & Deletion

You have the right to correct inaccurate information and request deletion of your data (subject to legal retention obligations).

8.3 Objection & Restriction

You have the right to object to certain processing and request restriction of data use.

8.4 Withdrawal of Consent

Where we process data based on your consent, you can withdraw that consent at any time.

8.5 Filing a Complaint

You have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

9. Security

9.1 Security Measures

We implement comprehensive security measures:

  • HTTPS/TLS encryption for all data in transit
  • Password hashing using industry-standard algorithms
  • Role-based access controls for our team
  • Regular security testing and vulnerability assessments
  • Secure authentication tokens with expiration

9.2 Limitations

No security system is 100% secure. While we implement robust protections, we cannot guarantee absolute security. You use the App at your own risk.

9.3 Data Breach Notification

In the event of a confirmed data breach affecting your information, we will:

  • Notify affected users without unreasonable delay
  • Provide details of the breach and data involved
  • Offer guidance on protective measures
  • Comply with all legal notification requirements

10. Children's Privacy

The Tapin App is not intended for use by children under the age of 13 (or the applicable age of digital consent in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that a child has provided us with personal information, we will delete such information and terminate the child's account.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, technology, legal requirements, or other factors. We will notify you of any material changes by:

  • Updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy
  • Sending you a notification through the App
  • Requesting your consent if required by law

Your continued use of the App after changes indicates your acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.
