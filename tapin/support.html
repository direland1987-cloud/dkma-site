Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account?
Download the Tapin app from the App Store, tap "Sign Up," and follow the prompts to create your account using your email address.
How do I delete my account?
Go to Settings → Account → Delete Account. Your data will be permanently removed within 30 days. You can also email us at tapin@dkma.online to request account deletion.
Is my biometric data (Face ID) safe?
Yes! Your Face ID and biometric data is processed entirely on your device. We never collect, store, or transmit your biometric information to our servers.
How do I join a gym/club?
In the app, navigate to Settings → Switch Club → Join Club. Search for your gym and request to join. The gym admin will need to approve your request.
What is Tapin Premium?
Tapin Premium ($3.99/month or $39.99/year) unlocks advanced features like unlimited voice notes, priority support, advanced analytics, and more. You can subscribe from the Settings menu.
How do I cancel my subscription?
Go to your iPhone Settings → [Your Name] → Subscriptions → Tapin → Cancel Subscription. You'll continue to have access until the end of your billing period.
Can I export my training notes?
Yes! Premium users can export their training notes and progress data. Go to Settings → Export Data to download your information.
How do push notifications work?
You'll receive notifications for class reminders, new posts, and important updates. You can customize which notifications you receive in Settings → Notifications.
I forgot my password. How do I reset it?
On the login screen, tap "Forgot Password" and follow the instructions. You'll receive a password reset email at your registered email address.
How do I report inappropriate content?
Tap the three dots (•••) on any post or comment and select "Report." We review all reports and take appropriate action.

  • Steps to reproduce the problem
  • Screenshots (if applicable)
