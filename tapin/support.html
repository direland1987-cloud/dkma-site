Frequently Asked Questions

Getting Started

Q: How do I create an account?
A: Download Tapin from the App Store, tap "Sign Up," and create an account using your email or Sign in with Apple. Follow the onboarding prompts to select your martial art style and set up your profile.
Q: Which martial arts does Tapin support?
A: Tapin is designed for all martial arts styles, including BJJ, MMA, Muay Thai, Judo, Wrestling, and more. The app is style-agnostic and adapts to your training needs.
Q: How do I join a gym or club?
A: Ask your gym administrator for an invite code or link. Go to "Clubs" > "Join Club" and enter the code or tap the invitation link.
Q: What's the difference between Student, Coach, and Admin accounts?
A:
  • Student accounts are for practitioners tracking their own training
  • Coach accounts allow you to create and manage classes for your students
  • Admin accounts are for gym owners managing multiple coaches and students

Subscriptions & Billing

Q: What subscription tiers are available?
A: Tapin offers five tiers:
  • Student Free ($0): Basic training journal with 30-day history
  • Student Pro ($4.99/month or $39.99/year): Unlimited history, media uploads, AI insights
  • Coach Solo ($12.99/month or $99.99/year): Manage up to 10 students and create your own club
  • Admin Pro ($29.99/month or $249.99/year): Up to 3 coaches and unlimited students
  • Admin Pro Max ($79.99/month or $649.99/year): Up to 15 coaches with advanced analytics
Q: How do I upgrade my subscription?
A: Go to Settings > Subscription > Choose Plan. Select your desired tier and confirm the purchase through Apple's payment system.
Q: Do you offer free trials?
A: Yes! Coach Solo and Admin tiers include a 7-day free trial. Admin Pro Max offers a 14-day trial (B2B sales). Student Pro does not include a trial, but Student Free lets you try core features before upgrading.
Q: How do I cancel my subscription?
A: Open your iPhone Settings > tap your name > Subscriptions > Tapin > Cancel Subscription. Your access continues until the end of your current billing period.
Q: Can I get a refund?
A: Refunds are handled by Apple according to their refund policy. To request a refund, visit reportaproblem.apple.com and sign in with your Apple ID. We cannot process refunds directly.

Account Management

Q: How do I reset my password?
A: On the login screen, tap "Forgot Password" and enter your email. You'll receive a password reset link within a few minutes. Check your spam folder if you don't see it.
Q: How do I delete my account?
A: Go to Settings > Account > Delete Account. This action is permanent and cannot be undone. All your data (notes, posts, media) will be permanently deleted within 30 days.
Q: How do I enable Face ID / biometric unlock?
A: Go to Settings > Security > Enable Face ID (or Touch ID). This allows you to quickly unlock the app without entering your password each time.

Training Journal & Notes

Q: What is "This Day Last Month"?
A: This is Tapin's signature feature! Every day, we show you what you were training exactly one month ago, helping you see your progress and connect past learning with current training. (Available with Student Pro or higher)
Q: How do I create a training note?
A: After attending a class or training session, tap the "+" button > "Add Note." You can write text, attach photos/videos, and tag techniques or training focus areas.
Q: Can I attach videos to my notes?
A: Yes! Student Pro and higher tiers allow video uploads up to 120 seconds. Tap the camera icon when creating a note.
Q: Why can I only see 30 days of note history?
A: The Student Free tier includes a 30-day history limit. Upgrade to Student Pro for unlimited access to your complete training history.

Technical Support

App Crashes or Freezing

Try these steps:

  1. Force close the app and restart it
  2. Restart your iPhone
  3. Check for app updates in the App Store
  4. Ensure you have iOS 14 or later installed
  5. If the issue persists, contact support at tapin@dkma.online with your device model and iOS version

Push Notifications Not Working

Check these settings:

  1. iPhone Settings > Notifications > Tapin > ensure "Allow Notifications" is ON
  2. In Tapin app: Settings > Notifications > enable desired notification types
  3. Ensure Do Not Disturb is not blocking notifications

Videos Not Uploading

Ensure:

  • Your video is under 120 seconds
  • You have a stable internet connection
  • You have granted Tapin camera/photo access (Settings > Privacy > Photos/Camera)
  • You have available storage on your device

Data Not Syncing Across Devices

Ensure:

  • You're logged in to the same account on all devices
  • You have an active internet connection
  • Force close and restart the app
  • If issues persist, try logging out and back in

Privacy & Security

Q: What data does Tapin collect?
A: We collect account information (email, profile), training data (notes, attendance), and usage analytics. For complete details, see our Privacy Policy.
Q: Is my biometric data (Face ID) stored on your servers?
A: No. Face ID data is processed and stored locally on your device only. We never collect, store, or transmit biometric data.
Q: How is my data protected?
A: We use industry-standard encryption (HTTPS/TLS), secure authentication, and partner with Supabase (SOC 2 Type II certified) for data processing. All sensitive data is encrypted both in transit and at rest.
Q: Can other users see my training notes?
A: By default, your training notes are private. Only you can see them unless you explicitly choose to share them in the community forum.
